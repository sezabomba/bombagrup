BOMBALYMPIC 2022

The BOMBALYMPIC event lasts for several days, Bomba Grup provides opportunities for employees to participate, and the competition starts at 16.00 - finishes. The first day of the celebration started with great excitement and enthusiasm. The BOMBALYMPIC event is expected to foster a sense of unity and kinship for all Bomba Grup employees, as well as increase fighting spirit and sportsmanship.
